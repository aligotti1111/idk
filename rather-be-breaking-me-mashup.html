A perfect blend of two massive progressive house anthems. DJ Wish combines Clean Bandit and Topic's uplifting classic with Retrovision's driving energy, creating a seamless dancefloor moment that captures the best of both worlds.

The Tracks

"Rather Be" by Clean Bandit and Topic is one of those tracks that transcends the genre—it's a song that works in clubs, on radio, and at festivals. The uplifting melody and emotional vocals create an instant connection with listeners.

Retrovision's "Breaking Me" brings a different energy—driving progressive house with relentless forward momentum. The two tracks share DNA in their emotional core and festival-ready production, making them natural partners for a mashup.

The Execution

DJ Wish doesn't force these tracks together; he finds the natural meeting points. The vocal hooks complement each other, and the drop structures align in a way that feels intentional rather than accidental. This is thoughtful production, not just two tracks playing simultaneously.

The mashup maintains the euphoric quality of both originals while creating new tension and release moments. It's designed for peak-time festival sets when the crowd is ready for hands-in-the-air moments.

The Verdict

This mashup exemplifies what progressive house should be: emotional, energetic, and euphoric. DJ Wish proves that when you choose the right source material and respect both tracks, you can create something greater than the sum of its parts.
