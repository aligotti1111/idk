Valentino Khan & No/Me — "What Can I Say" Delivers Heavy Bass House Energy

By Milo TressMarch 6, 20266 min read

Bass house heavyweight Valentino Khan teams up with rising producer No/Me for "What Can I Say," a relentless club weapon that showcases both artists at their peak. Released today via Spinnin' Records, the track delivers exactly what fans expect from Valentino Khan—heavy, hypnotic, and built for maximum dancefloor damage.

Valentino Khan has spent years perfecting his signature sound, blending bass house, trap, and breaks into festival-ready anthems. No/Me brings fresh energy to the collaboration, adding modern production techniques that keep the track feeling cutting-edge while maintaining Khan's raw, aggressive style.

The production is relentless. Pounding kick drums, distorted basslines, and minimal but effective vocal chops create a hypnotic groove designed to lock crowds into the rhythm. The breakdown strips everything back before building tension into a drop that's pure bass house fury.

What makes "What Can I Say" work is its simplicity—there's no overthinking here, just a straightforward club banger that does exactly what it's supposed to do. This is the kind of track that DJs will reach for when they need to elevate energy levels and keep the dancefloor moving.

Valentino Khan continues to prove why he's one of bass house's most consistent producers, delivering tracks that work equally well at underground warehouse parties and festival main stages. "What Can I Say" is another weapon in his arsenal.

Label: Spinnin' Records | Genre: Bass House | BPM: 128
