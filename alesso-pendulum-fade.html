Alesso & Pendulum — "FADE" Is the Progressive House Anthem We've Been Waiting For

By Jade Orion February 6, 2026 8 min read

Track Info

Artists
Alesso & Pendulum
Track
FADE
Release Date
February 6, 2026
Label
Capitol / EMI
Genre
Progressive House / Drum & Bass

Swedish progressive house legend Alesso teams up with Australian drum & bass icons Pendulum for an explosive crossover track that bridges two distinct corners of electronic music.

The Collaboration

This isn't just another collaboration—it's a meeting of minds that makes perfect sense once you hear it. Alesso has long been known for his emotional, melody-driven take on progressive house, delivering historic festival tracks like "Years," "If I Lose Myself," and "Calling." Meanwhile, Pendulum's influence spans multiple genres, from drum & bass to rock and beyond.

The Sound

Rob Swire's raw, raspy vocals carry emotional weight centered on a light that never fades. The track opens with a slow-burning build that steadily pulls the listener in, layering tension and space before Pendulum's echoing vocals arrive. The delivery is raw and carries an emotional weight centered around the idea of enduring connection and persistent inner strength.

As the song unfolds, Alesso's progressive instincts take over, gradually lifting the energy before erupting into a euphoric, festival-ready drop that's made for massive outdoor stages. This is the kind of track that hits just as hard in headphones as it does in a crowd of thousands.

Why It Works

The genius of "FADE" lies in how seamlessly these two worlds collide. Alesso's melodic structure gives the track its life, while Pendulum's vocals keep it grounded and raw. It's polished without being sterile, emotional without being schmaltzy, and massive without being overwhelming.

The collaboration was first teased via Alesso's Instagram, with a short preview following shortly after on YouTube. The hype was real, and the final product delivers on every bit of that anticipation.

The Tour

The track arrives alongside the announcement of Alesso's first-ever US headline tour, which kicks off at Colorado's Red Rocks Amphitheatre on April 25, with additional stops in San Francisco (June 5), Los Angeles (June 13), and Brooklyn (August 22).

The Verdict

This collaboration is the perfect combination of two artists at the top of their game. "FADE" is a statement track that proves progressive house and drum & bass can not only coexist but elevate each other. It's one of those tracks that you'll be hearing all festival season long—and you won't be mad about it.
