John Summit & Julia Wolf — "WITH ME" Continues Summit's Hot Streak from CTRL ESCAPE

By Jade OrionFebruary 27, 20267 min read

Fresh off the success of "SHADOWS" with LAVINIA, John Summit returns with another single from his highly anticipated debut album CTRL ESCAPE. This time, he teams up with rising vocalist Julia Wolf for "WITH ME," a tech house groove that showcases Summit's ability to balance club energy with crossover appeal.

Released today via Experts Only, "WITH ME" finds Julia Wolf's indie-tinged vocals floating over Summit's signature rolling basslines and crisp percussion. The track sits comfortably at 128 BPM, built for both peak-time festival moments and late-night club sessions.

Julia Wolf brings a fresh perspective to Summit's sound. Known for her work in the indie and alternative scenes, her vocals add an organic texture that contrasts beautifully with the electronic production. The result is a track that feels both underground and accessible—a balance Summit has mastered throughout his career.

As the third single from CTRL ESCAPE (dropping April 15), "WITH ME" continues to build anticipation for what's shaping up to be one of 2026's most important dance music albums. Summit has proven he's not just riding the tech house wave—he's defining it.

Label: Experts Only | BPM: 128 | Album: CTRL ESCAPE (April 15, 2026)
