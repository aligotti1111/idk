David Guetta & Hypaton — "Walked Away" Brings Emotional Progressive House Energy

By Jade OrionFebruary 27, 20266 min read

David Guetta teams up with Greek producer Hypaton for "Walked Away," a progressive house track that showcases Guetta's continued evolution in the melodic space. Released today, the track blends emotional vocals with driving production perfect for festival moments.

At 126 BPM, "Walked Away" builds gradually with lush pads and atmospheric elements before dropping into a euphoric progressive house groove. Hypaton brings his melodic sensibility to complement Guetta's polished production style, creating a track that feels both intimate and massive.

The vocal delivery carries genuine emotion, exploring themes of moving on and self-discovery. The lyrics resonate over the cinematic production, making this more than just a dancefloor weapon—it's a complete musical experience designed for sunset sets and emotional festival moments.

Guetta continues to prove why he remains one of dance music's most consistent hitmakers, constantly evolving his sound while maintaining his signature production quality. "Walked Away" is destined for Tomorrowland main stages and Ibiza sunset sessions throughout 2026.

BPM: 126 | Genre: Progressive House
