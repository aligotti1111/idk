DJ Mix / Tech House

2025 Send-Off Mix

LigottiDecember 202551 min

As 2025 comes to a close, Ligotti delivers a year-end mix that captures everything that made the dancefloor essential this year. Over 50 minutes, this set moves through the tracks that defined the sound of clubs and festivals, from peak-time tech house bangers to melodic progressive moments that reminded us why we show up in the first place.

The Year in Review

2025 was a year where tech house continued its global dominance while progressive house made a genuine comeback. This mix doesn't just throw together the year's biggest tracks—it tells a story about how the sound evolved, how producers pushed boundaries, and how dancefloors responded.

You'll hear the tracks that broke through—the ones that went from underground IDs to festival anthems, the remixes that surpassed originals, and the surprises that nobody saw coming but everyone played. This isn't nostalgia for nostalgia's sake. It's a functional set that works as both a retrospective and a proper club mix.

The Verdict

Ligotti's 2025 Send-Off Mix does what year-end mixes should do: it reminds you why the year mattered while still working as a standalone set. Whether you're reflecting on the past twelve months or just need over 50 minutes of solid mixing, this delivers.
