Hardwell, Vini Vici & MR.BLACK feat. Liquid Soul — "Crazy People 2026" Brings Psytrance Energy to the Main Stage

By Jade OrionMarch 6, 20267 min read

When three titans of big room and psytrance unite, the result is nothing short of explosive. "Crazy People 2026" brings together Hardwell, Israeli psytrance duo Vini Vici, and MR.BLACK for a rework of Liquid Soul's psytrance classic that's designed to dominate festival main stages throughout 2026.

Released today via Revealed Recordings, the track operates at 145 BPM—the sweet spot where big room energy meets psytrance intensity. Hardwell brings his legendary festival production expertise, Vini Vici contributes their signature rolling basslines and psychedelic elements, while MR.BLACK adds peak-time main stage sensibility that ties everything together.

The production is massive. Pounding kicks, hypnotic psytrance leads, and euphoric breakdowns create a track that builds tension masterfully before exploding into drops designed for hands-in-the-air moments at Tomorrowland, Ultra, and EDC. This is festival music in its purest form—unapologetically big, energetic, and built for maximum crowd impact.

What makes "Crazy People 2026" special is how it bridges the gap between psytrance's underground roots and big room's festival dominance. The track respects both genres while creating something that transcends either, proving that when top-tier producers collaborate across styles, the results can be extraordinary.

Hardwell continues his post-comeback momentum, consistently delivering tracks that remind everyone why he's remained relevant for over a decade. "Crazy People 2026" is destined to become a festival anthem, soundtrack to countless mainstage moments throughout the year.

Label: Revealed Recordings | Genre: Big Room / Psytrance | BPM: 145
