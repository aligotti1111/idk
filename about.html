ABOUT US

Oh It's Good is your no-BS guide to electronic music that actually matters. We're not here to chase clicks or regurgitate press releases. We're here because we love this music, and we think you deserve better than generic track descriptions and recycled hype.

Every week, we dig through the noise to find tracks worth your time—whether it's a viral festival weapon from GORDO and Reinier Zonneveld, a cultural statement like Ahadadream's Punjabi-infused club bangers, or an underground gem that hasn't hit your algorithm yet. We cover tech house, progressive house, techno, and everything in between, as long as it moves bodies and pushes culture forward.

We're not trying to be the biggest EDM blog. We're trying to be the one you actually want to read. That means honest reviews, cultural context, and a healthy skepticism of anything that sounds like it was written by a marketing team. If a track is overhyped, we'll say it. If an underground artist deserves more attention, we'll give it to them.

Based in the chaos of New York's club scene but keeping our ears to the ground globally, we track what's happening in Ibiza, London, Berlin, Miami, and everywhere else electronic music thrives. We care about the artists who are moving the culture, not just the ones with the biggest marketing budgets.

Honest Reviews

No fluff, no fake hype. If it's good, we'll tell you. If it's mid, we'll skip it. Our credibility matters more than label relationships.

Cultural Context

We don't just review tracks—we explain why they matter. The stories, the scenes, the movements that give electronic music its power.

Global Perspective

NYC-based but worldwide-minded. We track Berlin techno, Ibiza house, London bass, and everywhere else the culture is moving forward.

If it's good, we'll tell you. If it's great, we'll shout about it. Simple as that.
