2026 EDMA Nominations Revealed: Skrillex, John Summit, REZZ Lead Fan Voting

Published February 22, 2026 • Miami, Florida

Fan voting is officially open for the 2026 Electronic Dance Music Awards after nominees were revealed today. The ceremony returns to Miami Music Week with a new home at The Clevelander South Beach on Friday, March 27th, trading ballrooms for pool parties and bringing the awards back to their Miami roots.

Regarded as the nation's preeminent electronic music award ceremony, the EDMAs take place during the height of Miami Music Week and hand out unique trophies resembling DJ decks with functional jog-wheels. This year's invite-only event embraces a distinctly Miami atmosphere: no ballroom, no seated tables—just Miami, the music, and the people who live it.

Dance Song of the Year Nominees

  • Tiësto feat. Oaks - "I Follow Rivers"
  • Chris Lake, Skrillex & ANITA B QUEEN - "LA NOCHE"
  • CID & Taylr Renee - "Fancy $hit"
  • Gryffin & Kaskade feat. Nu-La - "In My Head"
  • AFROJACK, Martin Garrix & David Guetta feat. Amél - "Our Time"
  • Martin Garrix, Alesso & Shaun Farrugia - "Inside Our Hearts"

The nominations blend nostalgia with innovation, featuring both established icons and the artists carving out EDM's next creative frontiers. Skrillex, REZZ, and John Summit lead the pack across multiple categories, with Summit nominated for Dance Radio Artist of the Year alongside his recent domination of the tech house scene.

Known for delivering unannounced performances and "you-had-to-be-there" moments, past EDMAs have featured appearances from Above & Beyond, Afrojack, David Guetta, Deadmau5, Diplo, Dom Dolla, Hardwell, Kaskade, Martin Garrix, Marshmello, Mau P, MEDUZA, and Steve Aoki, among others.

This year's ceremony is produced by iHeartRadio's Sean "Hollywood" Hamilton and partners with Digital Music Pool (the official music source for the Electronic Dance Music Awards), EDM.com, and DJ LIFE Magazine.

Fan voting is now open at edmawardsmiami.com. The awards ceremony takes place Friday, March 27, 2026, at The Clevelander South Beach during Miami Music Week.
